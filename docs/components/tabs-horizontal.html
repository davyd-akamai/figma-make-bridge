TabsHorizontal

A horizontal tab strip — small/large sizes, optional leading icon and trailing badge per tab, disabled tabs, and a real measured collapsible-overflow behavior: when the row runs out of room, trailing tabs move into a "..." dropdown instead of wrapping or clipping.

Playground

Click a tab to select it. "Overview" has a leading icon, "Configuration" has a badge, "Access control" is disabled.

size

Collapsible overflow

Drag the bottom-right handle to shrink the row — trailing tabs are measured for real (not CSS-clipped) and swapped for a "..." button once they no longer fit, matching Figma's documented collapsible behavior. Click "..." to open the overflow menu.
